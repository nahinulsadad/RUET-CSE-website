Undergraduate Program

Program: BSc (Honours) In Computer Science and Engineering

  1. Admission: Students will be admitted to the department as per university rules.
  2. Duration of the Program: 4 years.
  3. Total Semesters: 4 X 2 = 8 (2 semesters a year of 22 weeks each).
  4. Total Number of Credits in 8 semesters (4 years): 160.0
  5. Breakdown of each semester (of 22 weeks each):
    1. Classes: 15 active weeks (1 day of each week must be reserved for makeup classes. If necessary, weekends can be used for makeup classes and extra/additional classes may be taken within the semester schedule to finish the course).
    2. Break/PL: 2 weeks. No separate break for in-course examinations.
    3. Semester Final Examinations: 2 weeks.
    4. Evaluation of Scripts and Publication of Results: 3 weeks (2 weeks for grade submission and 1 week for tabulation and result publication).
    5. Vacations: No separate semester break. Only the usual university vacations apply.
  6. Teaching of the courses:
    1. For each credit of a theory course, there will be 1 class per week of 1 hour duration.
    2. Total classes in a semester for each credit of a theory course will be 15 (15 X 1).
    3. Total Contact Hours in a semester for each 1.0 credit theory course: 15 X 1 = 15.
    4. For each 1.0 credit lab course, there will be 1 class per week of 2 hours duration.
    5. Total classes in a semester for each 1.0 credit lab course in 15 weeks: 15 X 1 = 15.
    6. Total Contact Hours in a semester for each 1.0 credit lab course: 15 X 2 = 30.
  7. Evaluation of the courses: The answer scripts will be evaluated by two faculty members, one of which is the respective course teacher.
  8. Grading System: The current UGC approved grading system applies as per university rules.
    Marks Letter Grade Grade Point
    80% and Above A+ 4.00
    75% to < 80% A 3.75
    70% to < 75% A- 3.50
    65% to < 70% B+ 3.25
    60% to < 65% B 3.00
    55% to < 60% B- 2.75
    50% to < 55% C+ 2.50
    45% to < 50% C 2.25
    40% to < 45% D 2.00
    Less Than 40% F 0.00
  9. Marks Distribution:
    1. For a theory course:
      Section Percentage of Marks
      Attendance 05%
      Assignment/Presentation 05%
      Incourse 30%
      Final Examination 60%
      Total Marks 100%
    2. For 9a (iii), at least 2 Incourse examinations should be taken. One near the 5th/6th week and the other near the 11th/12th week.
    3. The subsections (i)-(iii) of (9a) will ONLY be evaluated by the respective course teacher. Subsection (iv) will first be evaluated by the respective course teacher and, if necessary (case-7g), by the examination committee.
    4. For a lab course:
      Section Percentage of Marks
      Attendance 10%
      Reports 20%
      Continuous Assessment 50%
      Viva 20%
      Total Marks 100%

      In each lab course, some assignments will be provided which have to be solved by the students individually or in a group. The total number assignments given per lab course will be determined by the respective lab teachers. Each assignment has to be submitted during the lab time on the same day as the assigning date or some time later on a different day which will be determined by the respective lab teachers. For late submission, there will be some sort of penalty which will also be determined by the respective lab teachers. There can be bonus marking, if needed and felt necessary by the respective lab teachers.
