Admission

BSc (Hons)

Currently, there are five units through which a student can select the subject after appearing in the admission exams. These exams are very competitive as the best students from this country appear here to admit themselves.

To appear in the admission exam of a session, a student must fulfill the criteria of one of the following categories:

  1. Passed in the HSC exam in Science or Agricultural Science from any of the education board of Bangladesh

  2. Passed in the Alim exam from Madrasa board

  3. Passed in the A-level or equivalent exam with science background

A student can appear the exam of a session only if he/she has appeared in the HSC/equivalent exam in the same year. For example, to appear the admission exam for session 2015-16, a student must appear in any of the three aforementioned exams in the year 2015. Moreover, the minimum requirement for appearing in the admission exam is 8.0 by summing up the students’ SSC and HSC CGPAs after excluding the 4th subject.

After the admission exam, a merit list will be announced based on the score of the exam and the summation of credits of SSC and HSC exams. Note that, a student can be admitted in the department of Computer Science and Engineering based on the students’ merit position and two requirements that the student must separately get 18 in both the Physics and Mathematics in his/her admission exam.

However, the admission requirements are changed over years and are published in the university website. Please visit Admission Page for details about the admission of the upcoming session.
