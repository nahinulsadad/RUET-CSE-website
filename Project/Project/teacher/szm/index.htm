About me

I am Lecturer in the Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) department at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) in Rajshahi, Bangladesh. Before joining here, I was lecturer at Some University from June, 2012 to January, 2013. I received my BSc degree in Computer Science & Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) in 2012.


My research interests are computer architecture, VLSI design, compiler design, operating system, parallel programming, program analysis and verification. My research publications can be found in my publication section.
